About Us

Vidura College - Colombo is a pioneering Private National School guided by the Buddhist Philosophy teaching the National Curriculum in the English medium.
The unique characteristic of Vidura is how the school upholds the cultural traditions of Sri Lanka.
The School was conceived after an educational and market survey conducted in 1998 and it was decided to locate the school in Sri Jayawardhanapura - Nawala and Thalawathugoda, home to a large population of educationally enlightened parents.
Grades 1 to 4 classes are conducted at Nawala, Rajagiriya. The college campus at Thalawathugoda holds classes from Grades 5 to 13 (A/L). The Campus spreads over 9 acres of land, equipped with a playground, Basketball, Netball, Volleyball and Cricket nets.
The school's student body consists of over 2500 members.
The Vidurian spirit is what guides the children of the school to future success both in profession and personality. The strong culture of Vidura College prevails as its most unique quality of many, making it not just a school, but a home away from home to all its students and an institution of pure dedication to their development.
